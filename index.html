Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

Github: https://github.com/pauldennis2/ultimate-tic-tac-toe
(Not a web-app, not hosted on Heroku)

Description: Ultimate Tic Tac Toe (TTT) is a much more complex, somewhat recursive version of regular TTT. The Ultimate board has nine squares, like regular TTT, each containing its own TTT board. Players have to get three boards in a row to win, and your opponent's previous move determines your range of possible moves. I developed a heuristic algorithm for a computer to use to play the game intelligently.
